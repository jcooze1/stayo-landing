01

How It Works

01

Guest Profile Created

Guests build their Preference Passport — room setup, dining preferences, wellness rituals, lifestyle context — guided by an AI concierge that learns and refines over time. They own it entirely.

02

Profile Travels With Guest

When a reservation is made at a STAYO-connected property, the guest grants time-bound permission. Their preference context is securely transmitted — no repetition, no friction.

03

Hotels Receive Intelligence

Each department — front desk, F&B, housekeeping, concierge — receives role-adapted insight before the guest arrives. Teams coordinate with clarity. Service becomes anticipatory.

Privacy & Trust

Guest-Owned.
Permissioned.
Transparent.

Trust is the foundation of STAYO. Guests retain absolute control over their data. Hotels access context only when it's needed — and only for as long as the stay lasts.

Guest-Owned Architecture

Preference profiles belong to the guest. No hotel can retain, transfer, or commercialise guest data. It remains portable and deletable.

Time-Bound Access

Hotel access to guest profiles is strictly scoped to the duration of the stay. At checkout, access expires automatically.

Explicit Consent Model

Guests actively approve each property before arrival. No passive data sharing. No opaque profiling. Fully GDPR-compliant.
